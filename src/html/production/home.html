Welcome

Down

At the Sea Shell resort, nature measures time with picturesque sunrises, reflections of light shining through the boughs of the park plane trees, with the singing of birds and the chirping of crickets. Shrouded in greenery, this palace with phenomenal architecture and stylish interiors, delights its guests with comfort, modern punjabi culinary fantasies and professional service.

Wedding Venues

The 2 prime venues are the “Sea Shell" and the “Pelican”. Sea Shell Banquet Hall, our largest hall, with it's elegant decor in subtle tones of white and grey, complete with floor-to-ceiling windows, with a capacity of up to 600 guests is best suited for Big Fat Punjabi Wedding. Pelican Banquet Hall, which can accommodate 450 guests at a time, is an ideal place to host a more intimate gathering.

Catering

We know well, how important food and drinks are, in a Punjabi wedding ceremony. Our team of chefs and food decorators have over a decade of experience. With an exclusive set of dishes, comprising of International and Local cuisines, we'll help you create a customised menu, according to your specific needs, whether it is an intimate wedding ceremony or a Grand Reception.

Decor

First impressions are very important for any event or occasion. From table covers and drapes to creative lighting & artisinal flowering, we, at Sea Shell Resort, go into minute detail to create a spectacular atmosphere that will surely delight your guests.

Lush Green Gardens

Perfectly maintained lush green lawns dotted with numerous artistically designed beautiful gazebos provide the perfect backdrop for memorable events and occasions. Both of our luxury banquet halls are surrounded by beautiful green gardens sprawling over seven acres. If an outdoor ceremony or event is what you imagine, then our lawns can make it a reality. This beautifully landscaped area is framed by spectacular panoramic views and can accommodate over 2000 ceremony guests.

It is the celebration of not only two people getting married, but also the joining of two families and there is no better place to plan your dream wedding than Sea Shell Resort.
